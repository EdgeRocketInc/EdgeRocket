10 Stellar Learning Resources to Increase Your Growth

Growth. You want it. Bad. Here are 10 stellar resources to help you learn about all aspects of growthhacking, digital marketing, lead generation, and user acquisition.

Growth has always been an essential topic for businesses. But in the last few years, startups and other companies are focusing more obsessively on techniques to quickly and efficiently grow their users and customers.

Generally, there are two categories of growth-focused online learning resources that are available to anyone.

The first category is made up of new online learning sites and blogs that have emerged to help people build knowledge and skills around growth. These sites offer a variety of courses and articles from experts on key growth hacking and online marketing topics.

The second category is comprised of companies that offer marketing automation platforms. These guys have taken a page from their own content marketing playbooks (go figure!), and they’ve created excellent content about maximizing growth. The content includes articles, posts, ebooks, videos, slide decks, and certification programs. Most are high quality and free. (Of course, the educational materials from any given company will subtly -- or not so subtly -- promote that company’s product and perspective...so be alert to bias, and extract the valuable information.)

So that’s the good news: there are amazing online resources to help you understand growthhacking and online marketing. This content can help beginners master the basics of growth, or can help experienced professionals deepen their expertise. The bad news: you need to sift through the hundreds of options to find what’s most meaningful for you.

Start with this list of 10 stellar resources. Target what will be most valuable to learn. And get going with experimentation, implementation, and enhanced growth!

  1. Google

    As most know, Google Analytics is one of the most widely used tools for tracking website activity. Google has a library of educational resources to help you understand and use analytics effectively, including Analytics Academy classes and a certification test (Google Analytics Individual Qualification). Free.

  2. GrowthHackers

    A Q&A community site for growth hackers. Includes “Must Reads” and upvotes that help you identify hot topics and posts. Free.

  3. Marketo

    Marketo’s site offers hundreds of useful ebooks, guides, cheat sheets and other materials that address all aspects of online marketing. The SEO Cheat Sheet is a great one for beginners.

  4. HubSpot

    Like Marketo, Hubspot has a trove of resources about inbound marketing -- ebooks, webinars, templates, and more. HubSpot Academy has great options for training videos, projects, and certification. Free.

  5. Moz

    Moz is most directly focused on SEO, along with social media and link building. Moz Academy offers a number of superb videos (many featuring Rand Fishkin), guides, and other practical tools. Free.

  6. Growthhacker.tv

    Features video interviews with top marketing and growth leaders from exceptional startups and companies. Interviewer Bronson Taylor makes sure to provide specific, practical advice. $29/month.

  7. KISSmetrics

    Kissmetrics has a series of valuable Marketing Guides on a number of topics, with particular expertise in conversion rate optimization topics. Free.

  8. Zana

    Zana offers exceptional 1-hour courses on all aspects of startups. Their “Growth” section has terrific discussions by growth gurus such as Josh Elman and Adam Nash. Free.

  9. Quick Sprout

    QuickSprout University, created by Neil Patel, offers many educational videos that are conveniently divided into beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. Also excellent “definitive guides” to blogging, landing pages, conversion, etc. Free.

  10. General Assembly

    Dozens of 1-hour videos on Digital Marketing topics, led by a variety of in-the-trenches experts, including marketers from Fast Company, IDEO and the New Yorker. $49/month.

Other sites with growth-focused courses and content include Udemy, Lynda, Mixergy, DistilledU, Eloqua, MarketingProfsUniversity, and Copyblogger; YouTube, SlideShare, and Quora offer occasional gems. As always, you can use EdgeRocket to facilitate your search!
