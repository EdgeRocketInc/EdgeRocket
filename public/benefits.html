EdgeRocket helps employees learn new knowledge and skills, and enables companies to achieve higher levels of engagement and performance.

Employees

  • Search and compare online learning resources
  • Select learning content that best fits your needs and goals
  • Share ratings and recommendations
  • Track completions and certifications

Managers

  • Assign learning content and monitor progress
  • Manage “must-read/must-view” lists for your team
  • Organize learning resources for various roles and career paths
  • Support employees' quarterly/annual development goals

HR / Learning / Talent Management

  • Track learning and development across the company
  • Offer online compliance courses
  • Integrate with other company training content
  • Reinforce performance management processes

Company

  • Support employee learning and development
  • Save money on training costs and travel expenses
  • Offer a valuable perk that improves recruitment and retention
  • Make your company a “Best Place to Work”

...or Integrated Approach

Some organizations plan a detailed company-wide rollout, including:

  • Incorporating EdgeRocket with your company’s learning and performance management processes
  • Integrating with your LMS or talent management system
  • Working with EdgeRocket experts to plan a successful launch and achieve your objectives
